Bibliographic Information

Orchard Farming

by Baroness Elsa von Freytag-Loringhoven

Original Source

Encoded documents and images are derived from manuscripts in the Papers of Elsa von Freytag-Loringhoven in Special Collections at the University of Maryland, College Park, Libraries. In addition, individual pages or leaves of manuscripts are identified by the corresponding reel and frame numbers of the microfilm edition of the Freytag-Lorinhoven Papers.

Witness List

    Textual Notes

    Drafts of this poem occupy series 3, box 3, folder 14. The poem was written between 1923 and 1927. To the editor's knowledge, it has not been reprinted or published.

    Critical Introduction

    Critical Introduction

    Elements such as those represented graphically in this poem would not have presented well on the printed publication page. Indeed, it is quite often that, as the Baroness writes, "Print teeth bite."
